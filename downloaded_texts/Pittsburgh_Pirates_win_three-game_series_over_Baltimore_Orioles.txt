In inter-league Major League Baseball play, the Pittsburgh Pirates won two of three games against the Baltimore Orioles, the last game finishing up around 21:30 EDT today.

The series was even after the Orioles defeated the Pirates in the first game, 4-3, but the Pirates rallied back, assisted by two home runs in the eighth inning by Daryle Ward and Jack Wilson to win the second game 6-5. The final score of the third game was 6-1.
