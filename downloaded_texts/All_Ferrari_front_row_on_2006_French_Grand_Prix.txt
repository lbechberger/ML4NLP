Ferrari driver Michael Schumacher and his teammate Felipe Massa won the front row on the FIA Formula-1 French Grand Prix on the Circuit de Nevers Magny-Cours. 

Renault driver Fernando Alonso third. A very successful qualification for Toyota team drivers Jarno Trulli and Ralf Schumacher, both cars after Alonso.

French fans noted original aerodynamic elements on the Williams-Cosworth cars - the so-called "Petronas towers" owing to the team prime sponsor Petronas.
