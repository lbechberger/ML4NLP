There were 4 games played in the National Hockey League on February 22, 2008, all 8 teams being in a Western Conference race.


After a scoreless 40 minutes, Ryan Johnson scored his third goal of the season to give the Blues the first lead of the game. Teemu Selanne tied it up with his third of the season less than two minutes later. The score would remain 1-1 until 32 seconds into overtime, when Samuel Pahlsson scored his 5th goal of the season, and gave the Ducks their 8th win in 9 games. Manny Legace made 27 saves for the Blues, and Duck's goaltender Jean-Sebastien Giguere made 16 saves.
