The University of Oklahoma baseball team defeated Rice University 11-5 in the second game of the NCAA Super Regional Sunday. Oklahoma's (45-21) win over Rice (54-11) evened the series to 1-1.

Ryan Rohlinger led the Sooners hitting 3-for-4, which including two RBI at the plate. Offensively, every one of Oklahoma's starters had at least one hit.

Oklahoma and Rice will meet again at Monday at Rice's Reckling Park. The winner of this game will go on to play in the College World Series. The game can be seen in Oklahoma live at noon on ESPN.
