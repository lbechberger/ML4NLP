Second Ferrari driver Felipe Massa won the first place on the FIA Formula-1 Turkish Grand Prix on the Istanbul Park racing track. His teammate Michael Schumacher qualified right after him.

Renault drivers Giancarlo Fisicella and Fernando Alonso managed to get on the second row of the starting grid.

Jenson Button, winner of the previous Grand Prix in Hungary was sixth.

Nick Heidfeld had a nice qualification and will start before Button.

Kimi Räikkönen and Robert Kubica closed the first eight places on the grid
