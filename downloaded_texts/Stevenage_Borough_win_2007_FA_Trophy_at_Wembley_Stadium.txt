Stevenage Borough beat Kidderminster Harriers 3:2 in the final of the FA Trophy after being 2:0 down at half time.

The game was the first competitive club match to be played at the new Wembley Stadium.

James Constable scored the first goal after 31 minutes, and a second one at the end 
of the first half. Mitchell Cole scored the first goal for Stevenage 5 minutes into 
the second half, then Craig Dobson equalized. Steve Morison scored the winning goal 
2 minutes before the end of normal time.
