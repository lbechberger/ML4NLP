Chelsea FC and Jose Mourinho have "parted company" today. This coming after Chelsea FC drew with Rosenborg in UEFA Champions League yesterday.

Chelsea FC are 5th this season after a loss to Aston Villa and a draw to Blackburn Rovers in their last 2 results leading up to last night's result.

Chelsea FC plays Manchester United on Sunday. It's expected that either Assistant Coach Steve Clarke or Director of Soccer Avram Grant will fill in on an interm basis.
