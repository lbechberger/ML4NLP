Traralgon will host Moe on Saturday at the Traralgon Recreation Reserve and Showground instead of the originally scheduled Sunday. The contest wil be part of the Gippsland Football League's tenth round.

In an email sent out to media, General Manager Chris Soumilas said, "The round 10 Fixture [sic] of Traralgon Vs Moe has been changed to Saturday. By mutual agreement Traralgon will host Moe on Saturday 19/06/10." Soumilas followed up with an email to media and the league's clubs confirming the move.

In other round ten match up's, bottom placed Warragul will play Morwell, Maffra will host Drouin and Leongatha will host Wonthaggi in a night game.

The Gippsland Football League is the only major Australian rules football competition in the Gippsland region according to the Victorian Country Football League.
