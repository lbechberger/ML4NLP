The National Basketball Association (NBA) has released the rosters for the 2007 NBA All-Star Game. The starting rosters can be found on the newly launched 2007 All-Star Game website. The 56th All-Star Game is scheduled to take place on Sunday, February 18 at the Thomas &amp; Mack Center in Las Vegas, Nevada.

2007 Eastern Conference All-Stars Starters
:*Gilbert Arenas (Washington)
:*Chris Bosh (Toronto)
:*LeBron James (Cleveland)
:*Shaquille O'Neal (Miami)
:*Dwayne Wade (Miami)

2007 Western Conference All-Stars Starters
:*Kobe Bryant (L.A. Lakers)
:*Tim Duncan (San Antonio)
:*Kevin Garnett (Minnesota)
:*Tracy McGrady (Houston)
:*Yao Ming (Houston)

The All Star Website was also launched at about noon EST on January 26. The website features pictures of the players on the rosters and other information.
