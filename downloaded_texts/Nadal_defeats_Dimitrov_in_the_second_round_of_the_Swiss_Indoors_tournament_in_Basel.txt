On Wednesday Rafael Nadal defeated Grigor Dimitrov 6-4, 4–6, 6–3, to make it to the third round of the Swiss Indoor tournament in Basel. 
Nadal, who is ranked world number seven in men's tennis, won the first set 6–4 in 42 minutes. Dimitrov grabbed the second set 6–4, hitting 3 aces. Nadal had only 22 unforced errors compared to Dimitrov’s 31 unforced errors. In the third set Nadal broke Dimitrov’s serve twice to win 6–3.

The match was completed in two hours and 12 minutes. This was Nadal’s 53rd victory in 2015.
