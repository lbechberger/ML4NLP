The Draw for the 2008-09 UEFA Champions League 1st and 2nd Qualifying Rounds took place today. 

The UEFA Champions League draws was done at UEFA headquarters. The 14 clubs entering the 1st Qualifying Round on will play the 1st Leg on either July 15 or July 16 the 2nd Leg being played on July 22 or July 23. The 14 winners from the 1st Qualifying Round will join 14 clubs entering the 2nd Qualifying Round. The 2nd Qualifying Round will be played July 29 and July 30 and August 5 for the 1st and August 6 for the 2nd Leg.
