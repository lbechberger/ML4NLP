There were 2 games played in the National Hockey League on March 23, 2008.


Daniel Brière had three points as his Philadelphia Flyers beat the New York Islanders 4–1. Vaclav Prospal of the Flyers had two points, a goal and an assist. Only one of the five goals in the game was not scored on the powerplay; Scott Hartnell scored five-on-five during the third period to make the scored 4–0 for the Flyers. Martin Biron was solid in nets for the Flyers, stopping 32 out of 33 shots, improving his record this season against the Islanders to 6–0. His counterpart, Joey MacDonald made 22 saves in a losing cause.
