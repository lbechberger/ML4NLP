Maroochydore came from behind to defeat University 21&ndash;11 in their week five rugby union match on Australia's Sunshine Coast. Trailing 8&ndash;6 at half time, Maroochydore kicked five penalties to one in the second half to seal the win.

 

In other matches, Caboolture defeated Nambour 65 points to 20 and Noosa defeated Fraser Coast 72 points to five. Caloundra had the bye.
